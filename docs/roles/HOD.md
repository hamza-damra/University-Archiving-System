# Role Documentation: Head of Department (ROLE_HOD)

## Role Scope
The **Head of Department (HOD)** is responsible for the archiving integrity of their specific academic department. Their permissions are scoped to their own department's professors, courses, and documents.

## Primary Responsibilities
1. **Department Management**:
   - Manage and support Professors within the department.
   - Request specific documents from professors when needed.
2. **Quality Control**:
   - Oversight of document submissions for all courses belonging to the department.
   - Ensure professors are meeting archiving deadlines and requirements.
3. **Departmental Reporting**:
   - Generate reports specific to the department's performance.
   - View departmental dashboards to track submission status (e.g., Pending, Approved, Rejected).
4. **File Exploration**:
   - Browse the departmental file hierarchy to retrieve or review documents.

## Key Access Points
- **Departmental Dashboard**: Real-time view of submission metrics for just their department.
- **Professor Management**: Tools to assist and track the department's faculty.
- **File Explorer**: Direct access to the archived files of the department.

## Scoping
HODs cannot see or manage data from other departments. Their view is strictly limited to the `department_id` associated with their user profile.
