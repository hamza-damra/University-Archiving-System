# Role Documentation: Professor (ROLE_PROFESSOR)

## Role Scope
The **Professor** is the primary "producer" of content in the system. They are responsible for uploading the academic materials required for the courses they teach.

## Primary Responsibilities
1. **Document Submission**:
   - Upload required academic documents (e.g., Syllabus, Final Exam, Assignments).
   - Support for multiple file uploads for complex course requirements.
2. **Academic Organization**:
   - Manage documents based on their current course assignments for each semester.
   - Track the status of their own submissions.
3. **Personal Archive Management**:
   - Browse their own uploaded documents using the integrated File Explorer.
   - Access and preview previously uploaded files.
4. **Notifications**:
   - Receive and respond to requests for documents from the HOD or Deanship.

## Key Access Points
- **Course Dashboard**: View assigned courses for the current semester and their respective submission progress.
- **Upload Interface**: Modern, drag-and-drop enabled tool for submitting files.
- **File Explorer**: Professional view of all files they have contributed to the university archive.

## Scoping
Professors can only see their own assignments and their own uploaded files. They do not have access to other professors' data or departmental-level administrative tools.
